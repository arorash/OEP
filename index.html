What We DO

CAREER COUNSELLING

Every job seeker registered with us will go through a individual career counselling session with our counsellor. This session will provide us required understanding about the job seeker's mindset and career aspirations to assist them better in job placement and career progression. A job seeker may take these counselling sessions any number of times as required for rest of his lifetime at no additional cost.

Professional training

We are going to conduct professional trainings (Skill Development Training, Personality Development Classes and Spoken English classes) for every individual job seeker from our listed Institutions and Training Partners if Human Resource professional recommends for the same.

Personality development classes

We are conducting professional training to every individual job seeker if human resource Manager guided for that from our listed institution we are paid stipend for that. And after training we are liable to place them otherwise we are paying job less allowance.

Job Guarantee

If a Jobseeker doesn’t get job within 100 days after his/her training, full refund will be provided.

Terms & Condition for job less Allowance

  • You must attend career counselling after registering with us and before taking any interview.
  • You will be aligned for 30 interview appointments with in 100 Days after career counselling.
  • You must attend 25 interviews out of 30 interviews.
  • Your job aspiration must be realistic as per your skills and qualification.
  • You must visit interview as per guided or advised dress up for interviews.
  • You are supposed to suggest us 5 cities as per your choice for job recommendations. Minimum 2 cities from category C, 2 cities from category B and 1 city from category A cities.
  • If our internal HR recommends for any professional training, you must attend or join the training.
  • If our internal HR suggests you for any personality development classes, spoken English classes or resume writing to improve your skills and present ability, you must adopt the suggestion to ensure we can serve you smoothly and inline with our goal of your placement in companies.
  • Personality and Interview skill sessions are provided free of cost. You need to attend them if required/suggested by HR or Counsellor.
  • If 5 HRs (Who have taken your interviews) suggest you for lower position, your next interview will be aligned for lower position.
  • If 5 HRs (Who have taken your interviews) suggest for lower salary then we need to suggest you to go for lower salary.
  • If you are employed on a job, doing part time work or a side bussiness or you have any source of income apart from looking for job, or you are getting any government benefit for unemployment then you are not entitled for Job Less Allowance.
  • Jobless Allowance is Rs. 2000/- per month till you get the job.
  • You as a job seeker must obey all the Terms & Conditions mentioned above to avail Job Less Allowance from Employment Express.
  • If company needs to change any Terms and Conditions as per circumstances, Company has the right to change, alter or terminate any rule or Term & Condition without any prior notice.

Rules & Regulation for Job loss allowance

  • You must be a registered job seeker to avail job loss allowance and registration should be 365 days prior to avail Job Loss Allowance.
  • You must give notice to Employment Express before 15 days of leaving your present Job.
  • You must update your Resume every month.
  • You need to attend interview after 1 week of leaving the previous job and you need to attend minimum 2 interviews every week.
  • We will align you for 10 Interviews in next one month and you have to take 8 interviews out of 10. If you still don't get job after 30 days you will get job loss allowance from us.
  • If new job is fit and fine as per your resume, you must join.
  • Your job requirements must be realistic.
  • Salary demand should be realistic and not above 20% higher than previous job.
  • Job loss allowance is 50% of your in hand monthly salary or maximum up to Rs. 25,000/- per month which ever is lower.
  • This allowance is not a Government aid, it's only a social responsibility as a Employment Service Provider. So you need to join our referred organization otherwise you need to return job loss allowance.
  • You must obey all Terms & Conditions mentioned above for Job Loss Allowance. If you do not follow above rules and regulations, your registration will be cancelled & your all services and benefits from Employment Express will be Stopped/forfeited.
  • In special case of taking gap from employment you must take prior approval from Employment Express to make sure that your registration is not cancelled.
  • If company needs to change any term & condition as per any circumstance,company has the right to change,alter or terminate them without any prior notice.
